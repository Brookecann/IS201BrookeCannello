Addional Education

More detail on early education towards Brooke Cannello's Career

After graduating from Sand Creek High School on the honor roll, Brooke Cannello applied to further her education at a local college know by Pikes Peak Community College. She was unaware of what she wanted to study, but was certain that she needed to learn more so that she can one day be a helpful contribution to her community.

The various classes she took there prepared her for university level learning in her future. Brooke Cannello took classes such an microeconomics, macroeconomics, college calculus, accounting, and english just to name a few. All the classes helped guide her to make a choice. For example, it was in her english classes at the college, that she discovered she would never want to write another paper ever again.

In the begining of her continuing education, her aspiration was to become a lawyer. However, after a political science course she took at the college she pursued something of more interest to her. One year into her efforts to find her passion she signed up for an accounting class.

This was a pivital point in her education and it lit her passion for finance and accounting. From there she took her knowledge and ran with it.

After applying to Brigham Youg University, Brooke Cannello had no idea she would actually be admitted into the university. To her surprise she recieved an acceptance letter to her email a few months later. She was excited to find a school that would enhance and help her learn more.
